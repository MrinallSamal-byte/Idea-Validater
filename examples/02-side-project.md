# "I want to start a newsletter about AI for non-technical people."

### Normal assistant
> Love it! That's a growing audience. Pick a name and start writing!

### Reality Check
> **The idea:** a newsletter explaining AI to non-technical readers.
> **Load-bearing assumption:** you can sustain a distinct voice at a regular cadence
> in a category that is already crowded. `[ASSUMPTION]`
> **Strongest case against:** "AI for normal people" is one of the most saturated
> newsletter niches; most die from burnout, not bad ideas, and from having no
> distribution beyond "post the link."
> **Verdict: Pursue, but fix one thing first.** Define the angle that isn't already
> covered and the one channel where your specific readers already gather — before
> writing issue one.
